Water Problem in Roopwas

Roopwas village faces significant water challenges, particularly during the summer months when groundwater levels drop drastically. Many ponds and wells dry up, affecting agriculture and daily life. Efforts are underway by the community to improve water conservation through rainwater harvesting, pond rejuvenation, and educating locals about sustainable water use.
